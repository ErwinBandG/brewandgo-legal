# Brew & Go — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 27 May 2026

Brew & Go (Pty) Ltd, operating the Brew & Go coffee shop in Blackheath, South Africa
("**Brew & Go**", "**we**", "**us**"), runs the Brew & Go mobile app. This privacy
policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

## What we collect

When you sign up and use the app we collect:

- **Email address** and a **display name** you provide.
- **Purchase activity at the till** — when staff scans your loyalty QR code, we
  record that a stamp was added and which staff member added it.
- **Coupons & vouchers** you have earned, used, or expired.
- **Push-notification token** (if you allow notifications) so we can let you
  know about specials and shop news.
- **Order information** when you buy merch through the app (which products, when,
  payment status). Payment card details are handled directly by our payment
  processor (Yoco) — we never see your card number.
- **Photos** you choose to upload when an admin posts a special or news item
  with an image (admin-only).

We **do not** collect your location, your contacts, your browsing history outside
the app, or any personal data beyond what is listed above.

## How we use it

- To run the loyalty program (stamps, coupons, vouchers).
- To send you notifications about specials and shop news (you can turn this off
  in your phone settings).
- To process and fulfil merch orders.
- To respond to support requests you send us.

We do **not** sell your data to anyone. We do not share it with third parties
except where strictly needed to provide the service:

- **Supabase** — our backend hosting provider (database, auth, file storage).
- **Expo** — the platform we use to deliver push notifications and app updates.
- **Yoco** — payment processing for merch.
- **Apple / Google** — to distribute the app via the App Store / Play Store.

## How long we keep it

- Account data is kept while your account is active.
- Stamp / coupon / order history is kept for **5 years** for normal record-keeping.
- Push tokens are deleted automatically if your device hasn't opened the app
  in 90 days.

## Your rights

You may at any time:

- View or update your profile from within the app.
- Request deletion of your account by emailing **info@brewandgo.co.za**.
  We will delete your personal data within 30 days, except records we are
  legally required to keep (e.g. tax-related order records).
- Withdraw consent for push notifications via your phone's settings.

## Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data
from anyone under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a material way we will notify you via the app or by
email. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the current version.
