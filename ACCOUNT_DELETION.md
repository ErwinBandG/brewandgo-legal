# Account Deletion

Brew & Go customers can permanently delete their account and all associated data at any time.

## Delete in-app (recommended)

1. Open the Brew & Go app
2. Tap **Profile** (bottom right)
3. Scroll to the bottom and tap **Delete account**
4. Confirm the deletion

Your account and all associated data are removed immediately and cannot be recovered.

## Delete by email

If you no longer have the app installed, email a deletion request to:

**info@brewandgo.co.za**

Include the email address linked to your account. We will confirm and process the deletion within 7 business days.

## What gets deleted

The following data is permanently removed when you delete your account:

- Your name and email address
- Your loyalty card history and stamps
- Any Wall posts you have made
- Your push notification token
- Your account preferences

## What is retained

Anonymised, aggregated transaction counts may be retained for business analytics. This data cannot be linked back to you personally.

## Questions

Email **info@brewandgo.co.za** if you have questions about the deletion process.
